## üìã PROMPT GUIDELINES

### **‚úÖ DO Include:**
- **Specific location/setting** (hotel room, forest, highway, etc.)
- **Time of day** (night, dawn, twilight, etc.)
- **Camera movement** (slow push, pan, dolly, track forward)
- **Mood/atmosphere** (mysterious, unsettling, haunting, atmospheric)
- **Lighting** (dark, shadows, moonlight, single light, etc.)
- **Format specs** (vertical format, 9:16 aspect ratio)
- **Style** (cinematic horror, film noir, atmospheric)
- **Color grading** (dark tones, blue/gray, desaturated, etc.)
- **Movement** (slow motion, slow camera movements)

### **‚ùå DON'T Include:**
- Specific text/words on screen (add in post)
- Faces of people (Sora struggles with faces)
- Fast-paced action (works better slow and atmospheric)
- Too many elements (keep it simple and focused)
- Dialogue (visuals only)
